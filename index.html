Welcome to Lucy's Blog!

Hi there! My name is Lucy and I'm a 2 year old Golden Retriever. This page is for sharing more about what my day-to-day looks like as well as some important things for everyone to know about me. My people made me an Instagram account to share updates.

You can check it out here!

My hobbies include, but are not limited to:

  • Swimming at Cannon Beach
  • Sunbathing at the park
  • Barking at squirrels
  • Cuddling

My least favorite things are:

  • When my humans are on their phones or computers (really any time they ignore me)
  • Sirens
  • Getting groomed
  • Rainy days

A day in my life

  1. Wake my people up bright and early
  2. Go outside
  3. Eat breakfast
  4. Cuddle until it's time to go on a walk
  5. Get my zoomies out at the park
  6. Nap, nap, and then more napping
  7. Dinner
  8. Play until bedtime
